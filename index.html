Values Initiative Dashboard

This project shows results of the 2018 and 2023 evaluations of SFSU departmental retention, tenure and promotion criterias' service requirements. These visualizations help to answer the question of whether departments' views of service have changed over time. The rubric used for evaluation had two categories of question displayed here: type of service and level of service.

The drop down menu allows you to filter by department type, and clicking the buttons in the header will take you directly to that rubric category.
